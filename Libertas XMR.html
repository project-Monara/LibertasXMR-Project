About LibertasXMR

Our History

When LocalMonero and AgoraDesk shut down, the P2P Monero community was left orphaned. Thousands of users who relied on peer-to-peer, no-KYC trading had nowhere to go. LibertasXMR was born from this void in 2026 — created by Brazilian developer Iury Lee as a direct response to the centralization and surveillance creeping into crypto exchanges. We believe financial privacy is a human right, and Monero is its strongest tool. This platform is semi-decentralized: we connect buyers and sellers, but we never hold your funds. No escrow, no KYC, no surveillance. Just people trading Monero freely.

Why LibertasXMR?

  • Direct successor to LocalMonero & AgoraDesk
  • No KYC — anonymous emails and pseudonyms only
  • No escrow — we never hold your money
  • USDT to XMR swap tab — no KYC needed
  • Semi-decentralized — community-driven and open
  • Respect-based community — scammers are banned
  • Multi-currency support with country flags
  • Private chat between buyer and seller
